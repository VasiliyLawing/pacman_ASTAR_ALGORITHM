Write a tiny template engine in a form of function:

fun parseTemplate(word: template, vars: List<Pair<String, String>>): String

So you can call it like:
val template = "<<name>> is the best gamer! He defeated  <<anotherPlayer>>."
val vars = listOf("name" to "John Smith", "anotherPlayer" to "Black Death")

val result = parseTemplate(template, vars)
println(result)

and get this on the console:
John Smith is the best gamer! He defeated Black DeathBlack Death.
